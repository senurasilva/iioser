Hikkaduwa Beach is a popular tourist destination since the 1960s due to its palm-fringed environment and enticing seas. Snorkeling, diving, surfing, and other water activities are available, as well as low-key activities such as picnics, swimming, and beach volleyball.

Things To Do In Hikkaduwa

Hikkaduwa has a lot of intriguing things for us to do.

Swim with Sea Turtles

Hikkaduwa is the place to go if you've ever wanted to swim in the ocean among massive sea turtles.Many sea turtles may be seen just hanging out in the ocean from the town's coast. They are not afraid of people and will frequently swim right up to you!

Watch Baby Turtles Hatch

Swimming with the adults is fun, but witnessing young turtles is much better!There is a tiny guarded turtle hatchery on Hikkaduwa Beach where turtle eggs are kept. Between the months of January and April, visitors to this enclosure may check if any turtles are hatching. They released the turtles out of the enclosure at 6:30 p.m. and let them dash for the ocean!

Go to the Turtle Hatchery

Adult and newborn turtles can be found here. These turtles have been saved, and in the hatchery, they are being nursed back to health before being ' released into the ocean. It is also a safe haven for turtle eggs before they hatch and may be returned into the ocean. The turtle hatchery is open daily from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m.
